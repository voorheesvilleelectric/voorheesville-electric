About Voorheesville Electric

Built on pride, skill, and honest work.

Ryan started in the electrical trade working for a local commercial electrical company, learning the work from the ground up. He later enrolled in the electrician program at HVCC, where he advanced quickly and built a strong foundation in the trade.

After choosing to start his own business, Ryan built Voorheesville Electric into a trusted local name by focusing on clean workmanship, honest communication, and dependable service.
