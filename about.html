Ryan's Story

From learning the trade to building a trusted local business.

Ryan started in the electrical trade working for a local commercial electrical company, learning the work from the ground up. He really enjoyed learning the trade and decided to take the electricians program at HVCC.

He went for a year, learned a lot, and advanced quickly. Because he was so far ahead in the coursework, he was often asked to help other students. Eventually, he was ready to move beyond the classroom and build something of his own.

Shortly after his father’s heart attack, Ryan came to his family with a plan to start his own business. They told him it was the right time to try. Three years later, he has become a go-to electrician in Voorheesville and the Capital District.

Ryan takes pride in doing his best work for an honest price. That pride is the foundation of the business.
