# Ryan Surprise Plan

## Goal

Build a website that makes Ryan feel proud of what he has built.

This should not feel like a Wix template. It should feel like a serious local contractor website for a business with real workmanship behind it.

## Tone

- Professional
- Local
- Honest
- Clean
- Skilled
- Serious without being cold

## Key story

Ryan started in the electrical trade working for a local commercial electrical company, learning from the ground up. He went to HVCC for the electrician program and advanced quickly. Shortly after his father's heart attack, Ryan brought his family a plan to start his own business. They encouraged him to try.

Three years later, Voorheesville Electric has become a trusted name in Voorheesville and the Capital District. Ryan takes pride in doing quality work for an honest price.

## Website emphasis

1. Real project photos
2. Before-and-after proof
3. Local reputation
4. Clean workmanship
5. Easy contact
6. No posted pricing
7. Mobile-first design
